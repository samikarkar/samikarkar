Biography of Sami Karkar

Modelling, Simulation, Innovation.

Sami Karkar worked at the Laboratory of Mechanics and Acoustics (LMA-CNRS, Marseille, France), at the Lab. of Climate and Environment Sciences (LSCE-CEA, Saclay, France), at the Signal Procesing Lab. 2 (LTS2, EPF-Lausanne, Switzerland), and at the Lab. of Tribology and System Dynamics (LTDS - École Centrale de Lyon, France). He also collaborated with several other research teams, both in France and abroad, and developed numerous industrial partnerships.
